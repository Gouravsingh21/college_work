Akshay Kumar dons blue skin for OMG 2, calls it an ‘honest attempt to reflect on an important social issue’

Akshay Kumar has started shooting for OMG 2, the sequel of his 2012 film OMG – Oh My God! Pankaj Tripathi, Yami Gautam are set to star in the film alongside Akshay. The original featured Paresh Rawal, Mithun Chakraborty, Mahesh Majrekar in pivotal roles.

Vin Diesel walks Paul Walker’s daughter down the aisle as she gets married, fans say ‘Paul would’ve been happy’

The daughter of late actor Paul Walker, Meadow Walker, married her fiancé Louis Thornton-Allan in an intimate ceremony. Meadow shared photos and videos from the ceremony on Instagram which showed that her late father’s best friend Vin Diesel walked her down the aisle. Diesel is also Meadow’s godfather.
